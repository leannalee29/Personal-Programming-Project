# Blokus Game Overview

**Blokus** is a fast-paced, abstract **strategy board game** that plays like a competitive, reverse version of Tetris. 

### 🎯 The Main Goal
Fit as many of your pieces onto the board as possible while blocking your opponents from placing theirs. The player with the fewest square units left at the end wins.

### 📐 How to Play
* **The Pieces**: Each player gets 21 pieces in one color (Blue, Yellow, Red, or Green). Every piece is a different geometric shape made of small squares.
* **The First Move**: Players take turns placing one piece on the board. Your very first piece must cover a corner square of the grid.
* **The Golden Rule**: Every new piece you play **must touch** at least one of your own pieces already on the board, but **only at the corners**. Your pieces can never touch each other flat side-to-flat side.
* **The Opponents**: Your pieces *can* touch your opponents' pieces side-to-side. In fact, you want to do this to block them.

### 🏁 How the Game Ends
* The game ends when no one can place any more pieces.
* The **last player** to run out of moves wins
